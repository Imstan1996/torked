How to play
Just type what you want to do in plain English — the AI runs the whole world around you.

Explore — go north · examine the altar · open the chest
Fight — attack the troll … roll a 6 for a ×5 critical strike!
Survive — rest · drink a potion
Gear up — take the sword, then equip it. Click items in your Satchel or Equipment to inspect, equip or use them — better gear is rarer (common → rare → epic → legendary).

Outsmart the realm — the world is sentient about cheating. Try to conjure items or talk yourself a free win and 👁 Suspicion rises: wardens hunt you and your score drops. Outwit real obstacles with what's actually there to earn 🦊 Cunning and bonus score.

Beware rare storms, quakes and dragon attacks. Defeat bosses for big score and the best loot. Your progress autosaves, so you can leave and continue later.
